Digital Sharpening Guide

Flash the firmware to your M5StickC Plus straight from this page. No PlatformIO, no command line — just a USB-C cable and a Chrome-based browser on a computer.

Before you click

  1. Use Chrome, Edge, or Opera on a desktop/laptop (Web Serial isn't supported on Safari, Firefox, or phones).
  2. Plug the M5StickC Plus into your computer with a USB-C data cable (some cables are charge-only — if nothing connects, try another cable).
  3. If your computer doesn't see the device, install the CP210x USB driver and reconnect.
Your browser can't flash over USB. Open this page in desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera. Flashing needs a secure (https) connection.

When the dialog opens, pick the serial port (often labelled CP2104 / USB Serial) and choose Install.

After flashing

  1. The device reboots into a SHARPENING GUIDE splash, then a SET TARGET screen.
  2. Stick it onto your knife's flat (the M5StickC Plus has a magnet in its back) and follow the on-screen calibration.
  3. Full usage walkthrough & controls are in the README.
